Health Tracker


Reach personalized caloric/macronutrient goals

Summary

Welcome to the Health Tracker App, a comprehensive tool designed to help you manage your dietary intake and achieve your fitness goals. This app allows you to log your daily food consumption, track your macronutrient distribution, and monitor your progress towards your health objectives. Here's an overview of the key features:

  • Daily Food Logging: Easily log the foods you eat each day and track their macronutrient (protein, carbs, fats) content.
  • Interactive Pie Chart: View a dynamic pie chart that updates to show the percentage of protein, carbs, and fats consumed for the selected day.
  • Daily Logs: Click on a specific date to load and review your food intake and macronutrient data for that day.
  • Personalized Goals: When creating an account, input your height, weight, gender, activity level (sedentary, moderately active, very active), and goal (lose weight, gain weight, stay same weight) to receive personalized daily caloric and macronutrient goals.
  • Progress Tracking: Load a day's data to see a progress bar indicating how close you are to reaching your daily caloric goal.
  • Goal Alerts: Receive banner notifications when you're close to achieving your macronutrient goals for the day.


While this was a group project, I was responsible for creating the pie chart that responds to entered data, caloric progress tracking, enabling the editing and deletion of food item data from the UI, and goal alerts. I was also the team leader of the project and led major development choices.
