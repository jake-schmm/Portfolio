Document Editing and Social Platform


A rich-text editing and sharing web app

Summary

Welcome to my Document Editing and Sharing Platform, a versatile web application designed to enhance your productivity and collaboration. This platform provides a rich text editor with advanced formatting options including bold, italic, underline, strikethrough, font size, font selection, color customization, and hyperlink insertion. Here's a detailed overview of the features and capabilities of the platform:

  • Rich Text Editing: Create and edit documents with comprehensive formatting tools.
  • Speech-to-Text: Utilize speech recognition to convert your spoken words into text.
  • Save and Save As: Securely save your work with options to rename and manage versions.
  • Publish Documents: Share your documents with varying levels of visibilityâ€”public, self, or friends.
  • Download as PDF: Export your documents into PDF format for easy sharing and printing
  • Friend Management: Add and remove friends to build your network and share documents more effectively.
  • Interactive Forum: Browse, filter, and view documents shared by other users. Download documents that are public or shared by your friends.

Editor Page: Write rich text in an in-app editor, enable speech-to-text capabilities, save/save as your document, download as PDF, and publish the document in the editor with certain visbility settings (self, friends, or public).
