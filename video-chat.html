A room-based collaboration tool for teams

Summary

Introducing my cutting-edge Video Chat App, designed to make virtual communication seamless and secure. This application allows users to effortlessly join video chat rooms and connect with others by simply specifying a room name. Here's a comprehensive overview of the features:

  • Easy Room Access: Join any room by specifying its name and automatically connect with everyone else in the room.
  • Instant Video Chat: Start a video chat instantly with other participants as soon as you join a room.
  • Mute/Unmute Toggle: Each remote video (video of other users) on your screen includes a convenient mute/unmute toggle button for easy control.
  • Security Headers: Enhanced security with robust security headers implemented across the website to protect your data and privacy.
